## 2. The concept in one line
An **AI‑designed, self‑cooling neighbourhood oasis** that reclaims the ~6 mid‑day hours the heat currently steals, organised around the native **Ghaf** tree and Emirati **majlis** culture.

Five moves: **Cool Loop** (shaded ~1 km solar circuit) · **Majlis Grove** (social core) · **Adaptive Play & Sport** (all‑abilities, retains courts) · **Sponge Garden** (water‑wise, retains mature trees) · **Solar Canopy Spine** (net‑positive energy). Plus 4 identity gateways, market kiosks, café, prayer space, dog run, seasonal events.

## 3. Headline modelled outcomes
- Shade over paths/activity zones **18% → 72%**; comfortable area at 3 pm July **9% → 63%**; **+5.5 usable hours/day** in summer.
- Irrigation **−41%**; energy **net‑positive** (~165 MWh/yr on‑site solar).
- Buildable within **AED 35M** (~AED 2,300/m²), phased in 3 stages.
> All figures are modelled/illustrative for concept communication (grounded in Manual targets, climate norms, public site data) — to be firmed up with site survey + QS costing.
