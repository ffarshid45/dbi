# ZILAL AL SAFA — Submission Package Index
### Dubai Municipality · World's First AI-Powered Park Design Challenge · Al Safa Park 2 (Plot 3570616, 15,000 m²)

An AI-designed, self-cooling neighbourhood oasis — the first Dubai park designed, simulated **and operated** by an AI comfort brain. Built on a stack of **four benchmarked Dubai-first innovations** (the AI **Comfort Brain**, a sun-tracking **kinetic mashrabiya** canopy, a **net-positive solar** canopy and an interactive **"Every Step Counts"** kinetic floor). Concept + Preliminary Design, scoped to the AED 35M implementation budget. Deadline: 15 August 2026.

**Innovation & interactivity:** the full innovation stack (Comfort Brain, kinetic mashrabiya, net-positive solar, "Every Step Counts" kinetic floor, AR Ghaf grove, interactive water/light) is documented in `RESEARCH_INNOVATION.md`; the footstep-energy analysis and cost transparency are in `RESEARCH_PAVEGEN.md`. The kinetic floor is deliberately limited to ~100–300 m² hero zones and justified on engagement, data and safety lighting rather than kWh — the solar canopy carries the real energy load.

*All performance figures (shade %, comfortable hours, −41% water, net-positive energy) are concept-level modelled estimates grounded in the DM Neighborhood Parks Manual targets, Dubai climate norms and public site data — to be confirmed with site survey and QS costing at detailed-design stage. Winning-entry IP transfers to Dubai Municipality per Scope Schedule 6.*
