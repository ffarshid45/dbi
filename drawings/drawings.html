Design intent

A shaded Cool Loop wraps the whole park; the quiet Majlis Grove sits in the cool core; sport is retained at the SW end; the Sponge Garden lines the school edge. One identity gateway addresses each neighbour.

Planting palette

Signature & shade trees
Ghaf (Prosopis cineraria) — UAE national tree, deep-rooted, low water
Sidr (Ziziphus spina-christi) — dense shade, cultural value
Date Palm (Phoenix dactylifera) — avenues & identity
Damas, Neem, Al Markh — fast canopy & wind buffer
Shrubs & groundcovers
Desert Cassia, Tecoma, Leptadenia, Salvadora
Native grasses & wildflower meadow (seasonal)
Bioswale / Sponge Garden
Halophytes & rain-garden species tolerant of pulse irrigation

Water strategy

Xeriscape zoning · subsurface drip · AC-condensate & treated greywater harvesting · bioswales for stormwater · soil-moisture sensors. Irrigation demand −41% vs. a conventional turf park.
